About Us

My name is Linnea. I have been working with nails, lash & brow lifts since I became certified in 2023. I can proudly say that I am now Master-certified in lash lifts and brow lifts. You are always my top priority – don’t hesitate to ask questions or request anything! I’m truly passionate about this profession and always strive to learn something new. I stay up to date with trendy styles and techniques to always give my clients that little extra. My goal is to offer more services and eventually teach others in this amazing profession. I am so grateful and proud of what I have accomplished so far. I am trained in nails by Semilac and certified in lash & brow lifts by Vaida’s Lash Academy. I received my Master certification from Permalift. Find me on social media and check out my work in the 'contact us' section!
